TAGS

Tags are the hidden keywords within a web page that define how the browser must format and display the content. Tags work by opening and closing tags which create whatever you want to have on your webpage.

  • < body > The body tag is used for writing what you want to be seen in your webpage. The body tag has a property of creating the body for the webpage.
  • < p > The paragraph tag is to create paragraphs in your body. The p tag has a property of styling.
  • < img > The IMG tag is to put an img in your webpage. The img tag has a property of "src"
